Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BlockEstate

What is BlockEstate?

BlockEstate is a real-estate backed crypto token that allows anyone to invest in global property with as little as $5. We tokenize real-world properties, enabling fractional ownership and profit-sharing from rent, appreciation, and property sales.

How do I earn passive income?

Token holders earn passive income through staking rewards (12% APY), profit distribution from rental income, property appreciation, and revenue from property flipping. All profits are automatically distributed to stakers based on their holdings.

Is my investment safe?

Yes. All smart contracts are audited by leading security firms. Every property is verified with public documentation. The team is fully doxxed, and all treasury holdings are visible on-chain. Plus, community governance ensures transparency in all major decisions.

How can I buy $BEST tokens?

$BEST will be available on major DEXs at launch. Simply connect your wallet, swap your crypto for $BEST, and start staking to earn rewards. Detailed buying guides will be available on our Telegram and website.
